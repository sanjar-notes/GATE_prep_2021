====== 7. Expected Value ======
Created Saturday 30 January 2021

The **average value** of the RV, over a series of trials.
* For DRV → ΣX*f(X)
* For CRV → _{c1}∫^{c2}x*f(X)dX

Intuitively, Expected Value = (average value obtained in a single trial) * n
