====== 1. Number system ======
Created Monday 01 February 2021

=== Base Systems ===
* Binary: radix = 2, symbols = {0, 1}
* Decimal: radix=10, symbols = {0 to 9}
* Octal: radix=8, symbols = {0 to 7}
* Hexadecimal: radix=16, symbols = {0 to 10, A, B, C, D, E, F}, case insensitive.
