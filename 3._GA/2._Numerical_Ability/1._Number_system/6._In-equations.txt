====== 6. In-equations ======
Created Thursday 04 February 2021

There are 5:
* ≠
* >
* ≥
* <
* ≤
=== Properties of in-equations ===
1. Adding, subtracting same quantities on both side has no effect.
2. Multiplication/Division by positive quantities is has no effect.
3. Multiplication/Division by negative number changes sign.
4. Two inequalities can be added/subtracted, provided order of inequality is respected.
