====== 7. Cyclicity ======
Created Thursday 04 February 2021

This is used for calculating unit digits after long calculations.

=== Cyclicity of numbers ===
* 0 → **0**
* 1 → **1**
* 2 → 2, 4, 8, 6
* 3 → 3, 9, 1
* 4 → 4, 6
* 5 → **5**
* 6 → **6**
* 7 → 7, 9, 3, 1
* 8 → 8, 4, 2, 6
* 9 → 9, 1
