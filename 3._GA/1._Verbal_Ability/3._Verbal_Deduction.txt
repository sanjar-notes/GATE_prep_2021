====== 3. Verbal Deduction ======
Created Tuesday 02 February 2021

This is just to check whether the student interprets the paragraph correctly to arrive at conclusions.

**Note: Don't use real life facts. The paragraphs don't assume extra knowledge of anything, so take them literally.**

Tricks:
* Read the paragraph carefully. Then skim once.
* Find a keyword for the question. Not applicable always.
* Try to read the sentence backwards, especially if there's nesting or circular references.
* Elimination
