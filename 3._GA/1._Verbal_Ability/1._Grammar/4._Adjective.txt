====== 4. Adjective ======
Created Tuesday 02 February 2021

A word that describes quality or quantity or nature of a noun is called an adjective.

Types of adjective:
1. Quality - large, small, good
2. Quantity - many, some, little, more
3. Number - 1, 2, 3, 987
4. Demonstrative - This, that, these, those
5. Interrogative - what, which, whose etc
