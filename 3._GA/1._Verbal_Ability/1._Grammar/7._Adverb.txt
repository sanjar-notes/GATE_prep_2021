====== 7. Adverb ======
Created Tuesday 02 February 2021

A word used to modify the meaning of a verb, an adjective or another adverb.
Examples - Bolt runs //quickly//.
				The tea was //mildy// hot.

Types of adverbs:
1. Of time - late, early
2. Of Frequency - sometimes, seldon, everytime
3. Of Places - here, there, everywhere
4. Of manner - fiercely, diligently
5. Quanity - some, too, more
6. Reason - therefore, hence, ego
7. Affirmation/Negation - certain, not sure
