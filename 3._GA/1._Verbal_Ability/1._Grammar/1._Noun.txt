====== 1. Noun ======
Created Tuesday 02 February 2021

A noun is a word for name, place, animal, action. Anything that is not part of the grammar keywords.

1. Proper noun - A name, i.e it must be somewhat arbitrary. e.g. Jason, Linux etc
2. Common noun - Denote a class of objects. e.g. Car, pen etc

* Nouns may be used in singular or plural form.
* Collective nouns - a gang of thiever, a clowder of cats.
