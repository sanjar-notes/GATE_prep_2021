====== 3. Pronoun ======
Created Tuesday 02 February 2021

* A pronoun is a "replacement for a noun". This is used to make speech shorter and concise.

David is a good student. David gets straight A’s in all
the subjects.

vs

David is a good student. //**He**// gets straight A’s in all the
subjects.
--------------------
There are many kinds of pronouns:
1. Reflexive - self referrence
2. Emphatic - pronoun with an additional word for emphasis. Example: He //himself// is going.
3. Demonstrative prounoun - this, that, their, those
4. Indefinite pronoun - Somebody, anybody, one
5. Distributive pronoun - Talks about all the things in a set - Neither, either, each
6. Relative - describes relation with the noun - who, whose, whom
7. Interrogative Pronoun - what, who, which, where
