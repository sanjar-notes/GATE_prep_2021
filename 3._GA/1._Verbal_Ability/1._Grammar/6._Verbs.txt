====== 6. Verbs ======
Created Tuesday 02 February 2021

These words specify an action being done.
1. Transitive(relating to an object) - reading a book, driving a car, speaking in the mic
2. Intransitive - reading, driving, speaks softly
* Gerund - the //ing// used to show something as an activity
* Infinitive - activities that are deemed to happen forever - love, care, try, remember, learn, hope. A 'to' is used before them.
