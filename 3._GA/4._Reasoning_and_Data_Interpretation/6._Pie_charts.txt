====== 6. Pie charts ======
Created Monday 01 February 2021

=== Piechart ===
A disc where sectors represent quantities that are mutually zero-summish.
{{./pasted_image.png}}

=== Doughnut piechart ===
Multiple types of disjoint data are represented in the same chart.
{{./pasted_image001.png}}

=== Ring Chart/Multilevel Pie Chart ===
It is used to represent hierachical data. The ones close to the center are the parents of the outer ones, in that sector. Area ∝ data
