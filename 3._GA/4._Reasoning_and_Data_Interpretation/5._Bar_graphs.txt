====== 5. Bar graphs ======
Created Monday 01 February 2021

* Bar graphs are used for visualizing data mostly. Each period on the X-axis contains n rectangles, for n different entities.

* If multiple quantities compete and are zero-summish, different entities are stacked on top of one another.
